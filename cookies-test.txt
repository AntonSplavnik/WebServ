  1. Open cookie test page:
  http://localhost:8080/cgi-bin/cookie_test.py

  2. Test flow:
  - Click "Set Test Cookies" → Should show test_cookie=HelloWorld and
  session_id=12345
  - Refresh page → Cookies should persist
  - Click "Update Cookie" → test_cookie should change to UpdatedValue
  - Click "Set Multiple Cookies" → Should see cookie1, cookie2, cookie3
  - Click "Delete All Cookies" → All should disappear

  3. Verify in DevTools:
  - F12 → Application/Storage → Cookies → localhost:8080
  - Check cookies appear/update/delete correctly

  ---
  Curl Test:

  1. Set cookies:
  curl -i http://localhost:8080/cgi-bin/cookie_test.py?action=set
  Look for:
  Set-Cookie: test_cookie=HelloWorld; Path=/
  Set-Cookie: session_id=12345; Path=/; HttpOnly

  2. Send cookies back:
  curl -b "test_cookie=HelloWorld; session_id=12345"
  http://localhost:8080/cgi-bin/cookie_test.py
  Should display cookies in "Current Cookies" section.

  3. Auto-manage (full flow):
  curl -c cookies.txt
  http://localhost:8080/cgi-bin/cookie_test.py?action=set
  curl -b cookies.txt http://localhost:8080/cgi-bin/cookie_test.py
